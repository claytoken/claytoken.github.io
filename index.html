Full Transparency.

Clay World consists of an honest and transparent community, which of course makes no secrets about the distribution of $CLAY.

Presale - 16%

Our project adopts a fair launch approach, ensuring equal opportunity for all participants during the presale phase. This ensures a level playing field, fostering inclusivity and transparency within our community.

Liquidity Pool - 14%

Our project allocates 14% of the token supply to the Liquidity pool. This pool serves as a reserve for trading pairs on decentralized exchanges, ensuring liquidity and stabilizing token prices.

Burn Wallet - 18%

Our project burns 1% of the 18% allocation daily across 6 wallets, ensuring investor confidence and increasing token value through reduced supply.

Marketing Wallet - 25%

Our project allocates 25% of the token supplyto the Marketing wallet, spread across 6 different wallets, ensuring investor confidence. This fund is earmarked for marketing initiatives, such as advertising campaigns and partnerships.

Reserve Wallet - 23%

Our project reserves 23% of the token supply in the Reserve wallet, distributed across 6 wallets to ensure investor confidence. These funds will be utilized for listings, giveaways, and airdrops.

Dev Wallet - 4%

Our project designates 4% of the token supply to the Developers wallet, ensuring market stability and fostering sustainability. This allocation supports operational needs and fuels ecosystem growth, enhancing community trust in the process.
